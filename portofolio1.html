Paw Paw

Di era digital yang serba cepat, pengalaman pelanggan dalam mengakses layanan petshop harus lebih mudah, transparan, dan nyaman. PawPaw hadir sebagai solusi digital yang dirancang untuk menghubungkan pelanggan dengan layanan petshop secara praktis dan efisien. Dengan PawPaw, pelanggan tidak perlu lagi menghabiskan waktu mencari informasi stok produk, bertanya jadwal layanan, atau kebingungan soal harga layanan. Semua tersedia dalam satu aplikasi yang user-friendly dan intuitif. PawPaw tidak sekadar aplikasi pencari layanan petshop—ini adalah platform yang mengoptimalkan pengalaman pelanggan dengan fitur real-time product availability, booking layanan online, dan informasi harga yang transparan.

UX Design

Problem

Many pet shop customers face challenges when trying to purchase products or use pet care services, including:

1. Unclear Product Availability

Customers often visit a pet shop only to find that the product they need is out of stock. This leads to a frustrating shopping experience and can decrease customer loyalty.

2. Difficulty in Checking Service Schedules

Grooming and pet boarding services have limited slots, but customers often don’t know when they are available. Without a clear booking system, they have to call or visit the store just to ask for scheduling information.

3. Lack of Transparent Pricing

Many pet shops do not display service prices clearly, requiring customers to ask about each service individually. This can slow down decision-making and make customers feel uncomfortable.

Solution

PawPaw introduces features designed to enhance customer experience and help pet shops manage their services more efficiently:

1. Real-Time Product Availability Check

Customers can instantly view which products are in stock via the app, eliminating the need to visit the store just to check availability.

2. Transparent Grooming & Boarding Schedules

With an interactive calendar feature, customers can see available slots and book appointments directly, removing uncertainty in service reservations.

3. Pricing & Easy Booking

All services are displayed with transparent pricing, allowing customers to choose based on their needs without having to inquire about each price separately.

Information Architecture

The PawPaw information architecture is designed to provide an intuitive and efficient user experience when accessing pet shop services. This structure ensures that customers can easily navigate the app, find the services they need, and complete transactions seamlessly.
